About me

Born in Gorkha and raised in Kathmandu, I am a thinker and a maker and a fun person to hang out with. I am a mechanical engineer with a deep and sincere aptitude for innovation and creativity. With a work experience of 5 years in CAD, I am trying to bring in a professional and technical dimension to my work.

I am currently working in Nepal Communitere, a Not-For-Profit organization, as a technical lead to bring, install and operate first FABLAB in Nepal with a focus on digital manufacturing and helping makers, entrepreneurs, students, DIYers, etc, in a best way I can. I am also a co-founder of Nepal Mind Factory Consultants Pvt. Ltd. which has been working in series of projects like digitization of Nepal for local governance, building designs, CAD support and mechanical designs, etc.

I love to bring the ideas from head to hands. I am highly inclined towards design and make things by human centered approach.During my free time, I am involved in arts, travelling, movies and hanging out with friends.
